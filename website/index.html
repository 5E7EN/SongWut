SongWut - A Free Song Identification Service

About the Service

SongWut provides an automated solution for identifying songs based on audio recordings. By simply dialing our service, you can submit a short audio clip of a song playing nearby, which our system processes to determine the song's title and artist. If identified, the information is sent to you via SMS. Wut!

How It Works

  1. Call the SongWut service number: +1 347-SONGWUT (766-4988).
  2. Follow the voice prompts to record a 7-second audio clip of your nearby audio.
  3. Our system analyzes the recording to identify the song.
  4. If a match is found, you will receive a text message (from 866-323-6463) with the song details.
  5. If no match is found, or an error occurs, you will hear an appropriate message and can try again.

Compliance with A2P 10DLC

SongWut is fully compliant with A2P 10DLC messaging regulations. All text messages are sent to users who have opted into the service by dialing our number and submitting a recording. Messages are strictly transactional and pertain to the identification of submitted audio clips.
